**Audience:** Auditors, Reviewers, Governance Operators

---

## Purpose

This guide trains human regulators to enforce governance boundaries
without escalation, identity entanglement, or drift.

It teaches **behavioral execution**, not mindset or belief.

---

## Core Rule

Governance precedes response.

---

## The Four-Step Control Loop

1. Observe  
   Detect pattern distortion without absorbing it.

2. Invalidate  
   Terminate incoherent signals early.

3. Emit  
   Issue corrective signal restoring clarity.

4. Re-anchor  
   Return to neutral state immediately.

---

## What This Is Not

- Not therapy
- Not emotional coaching
- Not persuasion
- Not authority assertion

---

## Common Failure Modes

| Failure | Result |
|-------|--------|
| Absorbing signal | Burnout |
| Delayed invalidation | Drift spread |
| Persuasion attempts | Authority confusion |
| No reseating | Escalation loop |
